Who are we?

My Skilled Guy is a collaborative association that allows young and old people to share their skills. It helps older people to get out of their loneliness and allows the youngers one to share their skills and discover new ones. Thanks to My Skilled Guy you can learn tons of stuff like grandmother's recipe, or how to use the last smartphone! Don't hesitate and sign up NOW!
